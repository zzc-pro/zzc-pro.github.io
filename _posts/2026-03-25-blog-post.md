Random Thoughts
======
<p style="text-indent: 2em;">Effort doesn't translate into outcomes. Catastrophic opening. I’m deeply jealous of other undergraduates who are graduating with published papers while I have nothing to show for it and there's no time left now. The PhD admissions have become insanely competitive. The interviewer who screeened me for an RA position told me that every applicant now has at least one published paper. I even saw a professor posting on Moments about ten CVPR acceptances, mostly from undergrads' first attempts. Everyone says it’s easy for undergrads to 'pump out' papers these days, but if it’s so easy, why am I struggling? Are they all just born geniuses?
